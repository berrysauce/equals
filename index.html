definition(s)

LGBTQIA+ is an acronym that represents a diverse range of sexual orientations, gender identities, and gender expressions. Each letter in the acronym represents a specific group within the broader LGBTQIA+ community.

  • Lesbian · Refers to women who are attracted to other women
  • Gay · Primarily used to describe men who are attracted to other men. Can also be used as a term for anyone attracted to the same gender.
  • Bisexual · Describes individuals who are attracted to multiple genders.
  • Transgender ·  Refers to individuals whose gender identity differs from the sex assigned to them at birth.
  • Queer/Questioning · "Queer" is an inclusive term that encompasses a variety of sexual orientations and gender identities. "Questioning" refers to individuals who are exploring their own sexual orientation or gender identity.
  • Intersex ·  Refers to individuals who are born with biological sex characteristics (such as chromosomes, hormones, or reproductive anatomy) that don't fit typical definitions of male or female.
  • Asexual · Refers to individuals who don't experience sexual attraction to others, regardless of gender. However, they may still have romantic, emotional, or spiritual connections with others.
  • + / Plus · Represents the inclusion of other diverse sexual orientations, gender identities, and expressions that may not be explicitly mentioned in the acronym.

These definitions are simplified. You can find out more here.

Many shorten this down to "LGBTQ". Generally, this term includes anyone who is non-heterosexual or non-cisgender.

charities

Here are some notable charities that support the LGBTQIA+ community.

ILGA World

ilga.org · Advocating for equality & LGBTQIA+ rights around the world

All Out

allout.org · Global movement mobilizing millions of people to take action for LGBTQIA+ rights and equality

Rainbow Railroad

rainbowrailroad.org · Helping LGBTQIA+ individuals facing persecution and violence find safety and freedom through relocation

Outright International

outrightinternational.org · International human rights organization focused on advancing LGBTQIA+ rights globally

Trans Lifeline

translifeline.org · Connects trans people to the community support and resources we need to survive and thrive

GLAAD

glaad.org · Accelerates acceptance for LGBTQIA+ people in all forms of media

Lambda Literary

lambdaliterary.org · Advocating for LGBTQIA+ writers by giving them the recognition and voice they truly deserve

It Gets Better

itgetsbetter.org · Nonprofit organization with a mission to uplift, empower, and connect  LGBTQIA+ youth around the globe

These charities are not affiliated with this site in any way. They have not paid for their placement, nor are they in a particular order.
