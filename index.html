standing for
equality.

we're all equal.

No matter how you look, where you're from, which gender or sexual orientation you have, we're all equal. We're all humans.

charities & organisations

Here are some notable charities that support the LGBTQIA+ community.

ILGA World

ilga.org · Advocating for equality & LGBTQIA+ rights around the world

lgbt foundation

lgbt.foundation · Advocating for a fair and equal society

All Out

allout.org · Global movement mobilizing millions of people to take action for LGBTQIA+ rights and equality

Rainbow Railroad

rainbowrailroad.org · Helping LGBTQIA+ individuals facing persecution and violence find safety and freedom through relocation

Outright International

outrightinternational.org · International human rights organization focused on advancing LGBTQIA+ rights globally

Trans Lifeline

translifeline.org · Connects trans people to the community support and resources we need to survive and thrive

GLAAD

glaad.org · Accelerates acceptance for LGBTQIA+ people in all forms of media

Lambda Literary

lambdaliterary.org · Advocating for LGBTQIA+ writers by giving them the recognition and voice they truly deserve

It Gets Better

itgetsbetter.org · Nonprofit organization with a mission to uplift, empower, and connect  LGBTQIA+ youth around the globe

These charities are not affiliated with this site in any way. They have not paid for their placement, nor are they in a particular order.

"Every time someone steps up and says who they are, the world becomes a better, more interesting place."

– Raymond Jacob Holt (played by Andre Braugher) from the Golden Globe-winning show "Brooklyn Nine-Nine"
